ALL ABOUT ENHYPEN

The Seven Boys Full of Refreshing Charm

ENHYPEN (엔하이픈) is a seven-member boy group under Belift Lab

Formed through the reality show I-LAND, they debuted on November 30, 2020 with their first mini album Border : Day One.

Members

Heeseung

Lee Hee-seung (이희승), more well known by his stage name Heeseung (희승), is a member of the South Korean boy group ENHYPEN. He was born on October 15, 2001 and is the center of the group.

Jay

Park Jong-seong (Hangul: 박종성), more well known by his stage name Jay (Hangul: 제이), is a member of the South Korean boy group ENHYPEN. He was born on April, 20, 2002.

Jake

Sim Jae-yoon (Hangul: 심재윤), more well known by his stage name Jake (Hangul: 제이크), is a member of the South Korean boy group ENHYPEN. He was born on November 15, 2002.

Sunghoon

Park Sung-hoon (Hangul: 박성훈), more well known by his stage name Sunghoon 성훈), is a member of the South Korean boy group ENHYPEN. He was born on December 8, 2002 and is the visual of the group.

Sunoo

Kim Sun-woo (Hangul: 김선우), more well known by his stage name Sunoo (선우), is a member of the South Korean boy group ENHYPEN. He was born on June 24, 2003.

Jungwon

Yang Jung-won (Hangul: 양정원), more well known by his stage name Jungwon (Hangul: 정원), is a member and leader of the South Korean boy group ENHYPEN. He was born on February 9, 2004.

Ni-Ki

Nishimura Riki (니시무라 리키), more well known by his stage name NI-KI (니키), is a member of the South Korean boy group ENHYPEN. He was born on December 9, 2005 and is the maknae of the group, as well as the only Japanese member.
